ShadowBorn sirf ek gang nahi — yeh ek movement hai. Ek family hai. Un logon ke liye jo akele feel karte hain, system se thak gaye hain, aur chahte hain ki koi unke saath khada rahe.

Hum 25+ logon ki ek community hain — jo ek doosre ke liye jeete hain, saath enjoy karte hain, aur zindagi ko ek team ki tarah face karte hain.

Yahan koi judge nahi karta. Koi ego nahi. Koi fake nahi. Sirf real connections, real loyalty, aur real brotherhood.

ShadowBorn ka janam andhere mein hua — par hum saath mil ke roshni banate hain.

BROTHERHOOD
KE RULES

01

🩸 LOYALTY & BETRAYAL

Gang ka koi bhi secret bahar leak karna ya apne bhai ko dhoka dena — permanent ban. Koi second chance nahi. Koi excuse nahi sunni jayegi.

02

👑 RESPECT & EGO

Kisi bhi member ko humiliate karna ya ego dikhana strictly banned. Yahan sab equal hain — rank power nahi, zimmedari deta hai.

03

⚔ CONFLICT RESOLUTION

Problem ho toh seedha baat karo — public drama nahi. Leader aur Right Hand mediator hain. Unka final decision maana jayega — koi argument nahi.

04

📱 SOCIAL MEDIA

Gang ke internal issues kabhi public pe nahi. Social media pe hamesha ShadowBorn ko strong aur united dikhao — koi internal drama nahi.

05

🌘 NEW MEMBER RULES

Faded period mein zero violations. Senior members se seekho, observe karo, aur apni loyalty actions se prove karo — words se nahi.

06

💀 PUNISHMENT SYSTEM

Warning → Strike 1 → Strike 2 → Permanent Ban. Fair aur transparent — koi favoritism nahi, koi VIP treatment nahi. Rules sab ke liye equal hain.
