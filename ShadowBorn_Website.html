☠ What is ShadowBorn

Darkness Mein Paida Hue

ShadowBorn sirf ek gang nahi — yeh ek movement hai. Ek family hai. Un logon ke liye jo akele feel karte hain, system se thak gaye hain, aur chahte hain ki koi ho jo unke saath khada rahe.

Hum 25+ logon ki ek community hain — jo ek doosre ke liye jeete hain, saath enjoy karte hain, aur zindagi ko ek team ki tarah face karte hain.

Yahan koi judge nahi karta. Koi ego nahi. Koi fake nahi. Sirf real connections, real loyalty, aur real brotherhood.

ShadowBorn ka janam andhere mein hua — par hum saath mil ke roshni banate hain.

☠ The Process

ShadowBorn Kaise Kaam Karta Hai

01

Invite Only Entry

ShadowBorn mein koi bhi khud apply nahi kar sakta. Sirf ek existing member tumhe invite kar sakta hai. Yeh ensure karta hai ki har member genuine aur trusted ho.

02

Recruitment Form Fill Karo

Official form fill karo — apna naam, city, social handle, aur 7 loyalty questions ke jawab. Koi shortcut nahi — sach mein, dil se likho.

03

The Faded Period — 1 Month

Pehle 30 din tum "The Faded" rank mein rehte ho. Observe karte ho, seekhte ho, aur apni loyalty prove karte ho. Koi rule violation nahi — bilkul nahi.

04

Dark Oath Sign Karo

Official Dark Oath sign karo — brotherhood ka wada karo. Loyalty, respect, aur unity — in teeno pe commitment zaroori hai ShadowBorn mein entry ke liye.

05

Reborn — Official Member

Agar Shadow King aur Right Hand approve kare — tumhara official "Reborn" ceremony hota hai. Tumhe Gang ID milti hai, Member ID Card milta hai, aur tum officially "The Born" ban jaate ho.

☠ Zero Tolerance

Brotherhood ke Rules

01
🩸 Loyalty & Betrayal

Gang ka koi bhi secret bahar leak karna ya apne bhai ko dhoka dena — permanent ban. Koi second chance nahi.

02
👑 Respect & Ego

Kisi bhi member ko humiliate karna ya ego dikhana strictly banned. Yahan sab equal hain — rank power nahi, zimmedari deta hai.

03
⚔ Conflict Resolution

Problem ho toh seedha baat karo — public drama nahi. Leader aur Right Hand mediator hain. Unka final decision maana jayega.

04
📱 Social Media

Gang ke internal issues kabhi public pe nahi. Social media pe hamesha ShadowBorn ko strong aur united dikhao.

05
🌘 New Member Rules

Faded period mein zero violations. Senior members se seekho, observe karo, aur apni loyalty actions se prove karo.

06
💀 Punishment System

Warning → Strike 1 → Strike 2 → Permanent Ban. Fair aur transparent — koi favoritism nahi, koi VIP nahi.
